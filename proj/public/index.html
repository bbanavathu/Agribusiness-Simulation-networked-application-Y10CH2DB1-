Introducing Agribusiness Simulation Game

Simulation game to learn the impact of changing weather on different crop yields

Reason behind creation

Purpose to learn the impact of events such as floods, drought, heavy rains etcetra on different crop yields through a simulation game. Players are to select upgrade options available to them to help reach the target yield for crops.
